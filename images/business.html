Teambuilding - Business and Communication

APPRENTICE CHALLENGE

(2hrs to 3hrs - Indoors or Outdoors)

Convince the BIG BOSS you have what it takes to come up with a wild and wacky shopping channel. Teams will compete in a pitch to win business based on an actual company brief. They will be hired and fired along the way by our BIG BOSS and his board members.

BODY TALK

(1hr to 8hrs - Inside)

Research proves that only 7% of communication is words. The other 93% comprises 55% Body language and 38% tone of voice. Mastery of body language is essential if you want to improve presentations, negotiations, sales-skills and the impact you have everyday.

COLLECTIVE SOLUTIONS

(2hrs to 3hrs - Indoors or Outdoors)

Teams rotate though fun and creative challenges, handing over the task to the next team as they go - only a combined group effort will prevail! A genuine test communication, leadership and delegation skills.

CRYPTIC CHALLENGE

(1hr to 6hrs - Indoors or Outdoors)

One of our most popular team building activities; flexible for any size group and ability, indoor or outdoor and offering superb activities that test every type of team skill to ensure a long term impact.

ECO HOUSE

(1.5hrs to 3hrs - Inside)

Groups engage with issues of environmental sustainability whilst experiencing a hands on, informative and rewarding teambuilding exercise. Equipped with blue prints and building materials, each team is given the challenge to design and build a model eco-house.

GIANT BRIDGE BUILD

(2hrs to 2.5hrs - Outdoors)

This team building project will require delegates to focus on working together and to apply the necessary skills in order to come up with the solutions to construct the bridge - planning, creativity and teamwork.

LABYRINTH

(2hrs to 3hrs - Indoors or Outdoors)

Based around a strategy board game, successful completion of the tasks within the 6 zones will reward the teams with chances to spin the wheel of fortune. With 6 - 10 different tasks in each zone, the teams must tackle as many challenges as they can.

MONEY BONANZA

(2hrs to 4hrs - Outdoors)

Great for larger groups - teams must use their fun money to take part in superb activities and games, selecting the best team member to attempt the challenge! Money Bonanza tests leadership, motivation and delegation skills, as teams battle it out.

TEAM TACTICS

(2hrs to 7hrs - Indoors or Outdoors)

A series of competitive games including photography, logical puzzles, mental tasks and artistic assignments. Team Tactics is an ideal activity for both indoor and outdoor events.
